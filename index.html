EXPERIENCE

Vineeth has over six years of experience in designing and implementing solutions on Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) platform. PLM platforms are used mostly by engineering teams that design and prototype products. The major customers are, for example, the automotive and aerospace industries. He is also an expert in building great user experiences on the web interface, that caters to teams in engineering, science, and IT.
