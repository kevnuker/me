NurseLens

Design Goal

NurseLens was designed for an AppJam competition conducted by Avanade and the Virginia Tech Pamplin school of Business. Avanade is a Technology Services firm that works closely with Microsoft Research. The competition asked teams to design an app for the Microsoft HoloLens platform as a solution for any challenge in healthcare.

NurseLens App facilitates expert oversight and support for EMTs during transport and prepare emergency rooms for the arrival of a patient. The mortality risk increases by 2.2% for every additional minute during the transport and handover process. So the main goal is to save time during transport and patient handover, as a result save cost also.

The road to design

How it works

A technician can put on the HoloLens headset at an emergency scene and stream what is going on to a hospital nearby. The Headset can be connected to the internet via Hotspot or a 4G connection. This would provide access to a nurse or doctor in the hospital who can watch what is going on and recommend specific treatment or medication.
