Persistent Registration for AR

Design Goal

In an Augmented Reality (AR) application, placement of virtual content in the real world is one of the most challenging process. Misplaced content could decrease the usability of the application, and reduce the sense of presence/immersion. The process of placing content permanently in the real world could be defined as persistent registration. In this project, I am hoping to develop a persistent registration engine, which will guarantee accurate and robust placement of virtual content over the real world. This is a work in progress; and a minimum viable product is expected by 29 November 2017.
